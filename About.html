About Amira's Nails and Lashes

Amira’s nail and lashes recently started this January 2024, it was founded by Amira Hassanien Badawy. She started as a call center agent, and later on she entered the cosmetics business. Her first experience is an esthetician as a side job and then resigned after having a few years of experience to start her own nails and lashes Salon and even today, she is still in the business. 

Amira’s Nails and Lashes is a rising nail and lashes service in the city. She has a good reputation for providing high-quality cosmetics maintenance and nail & lashes services after a few years of experience.

Her facility is outfitted with good equipment to handle a wide range of cosmetics products. Amira’s Nails and Lashes is a trusted choice for women looking for dependable and skilled nails and lashes services, whether it’s for an event, weekly or monthly routine. Home service and Home based studio are available in Amira’s Nails and Lashes. Amira’s Nails and Lashes operates between 10am and 7pm depending on the availability of the owner. we are located at 1361 Sevilla St.
